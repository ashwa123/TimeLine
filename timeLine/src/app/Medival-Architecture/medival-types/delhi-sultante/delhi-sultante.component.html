Malwa School
  • aka "Pathan School of Architecture"
  • @Dhar & @Mandu, Mathya Pradesh
  • Features:
    • Different Colored stones, Marbles
    • Large Windows [European influence]
    • Windows with Arches & Pillars
    • Stairs used
    • Absence of Minars
    • Batter system [receding slope of a wall]

Malwa school is the Best Example of Environmental adaptation

  • Large Windows [well ventilated]
  • Baulis [Artificial Reservoirs]
  • Lightly Arched pavilions [for cooling & airy]
  • Local available materials used

Jaunpur School

Bijapur School
  • Adil Shah of Bijapur
  • aka "Deccan Style"
  • Features:
    • Rich Carvings
    • Iron Clamps + Plaster of Mortar to give strength
    • 3-Arched Facade
    • Bulbous Dome [almost spherical]
    • Celings without apparent support
    • Introduced Cornices
