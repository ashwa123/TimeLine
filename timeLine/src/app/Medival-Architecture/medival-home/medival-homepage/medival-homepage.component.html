Mistakes to be avoided to score better:


  • Read the standard book once and use The Timeline Trends contents to revise.
  • Observe the picture carefully. Most of the time questions asked related to the aritectural details,
    like Stone colour, Height, Special features, designs etc.
    • For example, 2018 CSE question

    With reference to the cultural history of India, consider the following statements:

    1. White marble was used in making Buland Darwaza & Khankah at Fatehpur Sikri
    2. Red sandstone and marble were used in Bara Imambara & Rumi Darwaza at Lucknow

    which statement is/are correct?
    a. 1 only
    b. 2 only
    c. Both 1 & 2
    d. Neither 1 nor 2

    Answer: a
  • To answer this question just observe the below pictures, you can answer easily.
  • 1st Image: Buland Darwaza - which is made by redsand stone, but you can see the white marble which is used in the door and top for decoration.
  • 2nd Image: Bara Imambara - which does not have any redsand stone. Hence the second statement is wrong.
