About Us

  • This website is for those who are appearing for government exams including UPSC, SPSC and others.

  • Timeline Trends mainly focus on History facts, Art and Culture, Environment Facts, Maps etc.

  • We will be uploading our content continuously (daily or weekly), so stay connected always for various topics.

How to use this website efficiently

  • Read the “Tips and mistakes to be avoided” section before reading every topic.
  • Read the standard book whichever you like once or twice, and use our website to revise.
  • After reading the content once, just read the bold letters to revise. So that you can revise quickly.
  • History is a subject full of facts, so revise every topic whenever you get free time daily even if you are well thorough in respective topics.
  • We will upload different topics daily or weekly, so stay connected always.
  • Make good use of pictures, timelines, maps, colour differentiations for places, names in our website to revise quickly and remember the facts for long time.
